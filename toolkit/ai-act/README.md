# The EU AI Act, for the investment field

*What the EU AI Act means for the people who run capital — funds, family
offices, advisors, LPs — in plain language, written by a practitioner, not a
lawyer. Last reviewed: June 2026. The Act and its guidance move quickly; check
the primary sources before relying on any of this. Not legal advice.*

## Why this exists

Most AI Act coverage is written by lawyers for lawyers, or by vendors selling a
fix. This is the missing middle: what it means in practice if you run money and
your team has started using AI — what's genuinely required, what's noise, and
what to put in place before someone asks "who decided this?"
