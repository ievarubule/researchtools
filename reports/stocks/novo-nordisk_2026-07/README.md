# Novo Nordisk — research note, July 2026

**Thesis in one sentence:** at DKK 324 the market price is consistent with
roughly 0–3% long-run profit growth on normalised earnings — a below-peer-median
expectation for the group's second-fastest grower — but that reading holds only
if free cash flow normalises after the capacity build-out, and the note shows
both cases rather than picking one.
